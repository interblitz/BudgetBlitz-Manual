Preface¶

It would be nice to draw your attention to the common issue of a Budget Blitz for Android user. It is the high entry level. That means a user should have some time for studying the application. Therefore please schedule a time and it will not be lost for nothing. Most users say they do not regret about time spent.

Introduction¶

User manual will help you to get started with the app Budget Blitz for Android. The guide is not exhaustive but it is constantly supplemented and developed along with the application. Comments and suggestions are welcome, see. Feedback.
